About Us

Science Club is dedicated to providing students at Turner Fenton an opportunity to immerse themselves in the world of STEM. Throughout the year, we expose students to the areas of Engineering, Health Sciences, Technology, Physics, Biology, Chemistry. This is done through various workshops, guest speakers, events, experiments, and field trips, ultimately providing students with an enriching learning opportunity like no other.

In addition to our active-learning approach, Science Club provides students with amazing opportunities and internships in the fields of Scientific research, Engineering, and more! Our non-traditional way of achieving new understandings and connecting students with opportunities embraces creativity and lifelong passion.

We highly encourage you to come out to our Weekly meetings Thursdays at lunch in room 235. Not only will you leave our meetings more knowledgeable, but you’ll build lifelong friendships with like-minded individuals in our school community.
